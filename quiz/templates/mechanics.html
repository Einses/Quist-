Q U I S T

A quiz with a twist

Game Mechanics

  1. Before beginning, the player must indicate his name, age, and current grade. After that, the game commences.

  2. The player is only allowed to choose one answer to each question. Be sure to select the correct one.

  3. The player is given three power-ups at the beginning of the quiz. Only one power-up is allowed in each question.

  4. The participant has three hearts, indicating the lives of the player.

  5. Once an incorrect answer is selected, the player loses one heart. The game ends if there are no remaining hearts.

  6. Once finished, results are displayed along with the scoreboard.
